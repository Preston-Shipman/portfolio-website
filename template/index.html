I'm Preston Shipman

Hey there my name is Preston Shipman an aspiring software developer. I'm originally from Menifee California but the Summer of 2014 I moved to Yorba Linda in Orange County. It was at this time I attended Yorba Linda high school and took a Mobile App Development class where we were taught Jquery Mobile, JavaScript, CSS, and HTML. This was my gateway into software development and ever since then I knew I wanted to be a developer After high school I worked numerous jobs and while I enjoyed my time there I knew I had to keep my focus on software. This led me to finding freeCodeCamp which helped me get back into programming after a hiatus. I finished the Web Development section of freeCodeCamp but when the more advanced topics came I knew I needed to get help and jump all in. This led me to attending LearningFuze. LearningFuze Code Bootcamp allowed me to learn so much more than I could on my own thanks to the teachers there who could explain technologies to me in a way I could understand. They taught me many languages such as JavaScript, Jquery, React, and Node.JS. Now with many new skills under my belt I am ready to be a Junior Developer!
